Numus Financial is a venture capital partner with a difference. We are similar to traditional venture capital firms in that we assist our partners with access to capital and entry into the public markets. We are unique in that we go far beyond capital and listing – we work along side our partners. Our team supports the companies we invest in with regulatory, compliance, network and shareholder services.

We are resolute in support of our clients and we are proud of our industry leading return on investment success ratio. Numus is very selective in choosing companies to invest in, but we are equally tenacious in supporting companies until success is achieved. We don’t just want our clients to be successful, we help them achieve that success.

Who we invest in

  • High Growth companies with disruptive technologies or strong competitive advantages
  • That have proven, driven management teams driven
  • We are sector and geographic agnostic, but have an industry preference for technology, Life sciences, platform technology and disruptive businesses

Our Approach

  • We finance companies after an extensive due diligence process
  • We set milestones for success
  • We look for companies that wish to list in the Canadian public markets in the next 6-24 months
  • We lead the listing process guiding our partners through; Initial Public Listing (IPO), Reverse Take-overs (RTO), or qualifying transactions with Capital Pool Companies (CPC)

We Continue to Assist companies after listing

  • With regulatory and compliance issues
  • We provide advice and guidance with shareholder communications and provide access to our client/partner networks
  • Through our wholly owned Exempt Market Dealer (EMD) Numus Capital, we will lead multiple financing rounds as required
  • We can provide assistance with back office services such as financial, regulatory filings, compliance and communications services

We are picky

  • We only participate in a select number of investments each year
  • We are significantly vested with all our partners - Numus only works with companies that we hold a meaningful investment position
  • Only move forward on companies where our business philosophy align with management
